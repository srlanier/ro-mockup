TRANSCRIPTS

Official Transcript Ordering

Order your UGA transcripts securely via Parchment.

Summer 2025 Availability

  • Grades Posting: Posted on Athena August 5. Verify before ordering.
  • Degree Award: Awarded between August 5–18. Confirm graduation date on Athena.

Fees & Processing

Fee: $8.00 per copy
Processing: 5 business days
Delivery: 6–13 business days (USPS)
Sales Tax: Effective Jan 6, 2025 (shown at checkout)

Shipping Options

  • Standard (USPS): Included ($8.00), 6–13 business days
  • Expedited (FedEx): +$12 (US) / +$50 (Intl)

Online Ordering

Current Students

  1. Log in at my.uga.edu
  2. Click Order Transcripts under Instant Access
  3. Submit credit/debit payment
  4. Processing in 1–5 business days

Former Students

  1. Go to Parchment
  2. Enter email in Start Here
  3. Log in or create an account
  4. Submit payment & wait 1–5 business days

Mail‑In Requests

Complete the UGA Student Transcript Request Form and mail with payment:

Office of the Registrar
Holmes‑Hunter Academic Building
University of Georgia
Athens, GA 30602‑6113

*Use money orders or corporate checks only; no personal checks or cash.*

Third‑Party Requests

  • Third Party Transcript Request Form
  • Signed Permission to Release Educational Info
  • Money order or corporate check

Enclosures & Support

  • Upload electronic attachments online—they’ll be sent with your transcript.
  • Mail originals for notarized or non-copy enclosures.
  • Download notarization request for Apostille/Great Seal here.

Questions? Email reghelp@uga.edu or call 706‑542‑3106.
