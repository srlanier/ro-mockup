News & Updates

New Daily Class Schedule

July 14, 2025

Effective spring 2026, a new Daily Class Schedule will be launched on campus. The Office of the Registrar will be offering trainings to assist schedulers with adhering to the new policy requirements. The first training is scheduled for July 2, at 9:00 am on Zoom. Additional training dates and information are available.

Schedulers will be able to load spring 2026 courses beginning July 1 through August 31.

DegreeWorks Update

July 1, 2025

DegreeWorks updates are live and now include the addition of undergraduate certficates and other revisions to align with the launch of the new UGA Bulletin. Please be aware that some exceptions on active student audits are being re-entered and will show as being applied by the “DegreeWorks Manager.” Students can still view original exception information by hovering over the Details box.

Detailed new FAQs and tutorials about the updates are available on the Office of the Registrar website.
