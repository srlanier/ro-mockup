APPLY TO GRADUATE

How to Apply to Graduate in ATHENA

  1. 1

    Step 1: Log in to ATHENA

  2. 2

    Step 2: Open the 'Student Services' menu

  3. 3

    Step 3: Expand the 'Graduation' heading and select 'Apply to Graduate'

  4. 4

    Step 4: Select the Curriculum Term

    Note this is not your graduation term; it is the semester from which your curriculum will be pulled. Please select the latest term available.

  5. 5

    Step 5: Select the Program

    If more than one program is listed, choose one and proceed. A separate graduation application is required for each program, including certificates.

  6. 6

    Step 6: Select the Graduation Date

  7. 7

    Step 7: Select whether you will attend the commencement ceremony

    This option is only available for fall and spring commencement ceremonies. Summer undergraduate students participate in the spring commencement ceremony, and summer graduate students participate in the following fall commencement ceremony.

  8. 8

    Step 8: Review the diploma name information

    Diplomas are printed using the student's legal name on file with the University of Georgia. If a student needs to officially change his or her legal name with UGA, a Name Change Form needs to be submitted to the Office of the Registrar along with the required documentation.

  9. 9

    Step 9: Select the mailing address for your diploma

    You can add a new address if necessary. Diplomas are typically mailed approximately 8-10 weeks or so after the official graduation date. Please note that diplomas will be shipped via the United States Postal Service and should arrive at the provided address within 4-6 weeks of mailing if it is being delivered within the U.S. Please allow up to ten weeks for international shipments.

  10. 10

    Step 10: Review and confirm the application information

    If anything is incorrect, go back and make corrections. If all details are correct, click 'Submit Request.'


For students graduating with multiple programs of study, including certificates, you must repeat these steps for each program.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How can I view my submitted application?

Locate the 'Student Services' menu (Step 2 above), expand the 'Graduation' heading, and select 'View Application to Graduate.'

When will my degree or certificate be awarded?

The conferral period begins after grades are due and lasts 10 business days. Graduation Clearance Officers in the colleges will review the degree and certificate requirements of all students with an active Graduation Application.

Can I participate in any commencement ceremony I want?

Only those undergraduates who apply for spring or summer graduation are eligible to participate in the corresponding spring undergraduate commencement ceremony. Those eligible for spring or summer commencement are not eligible to participate in any other future commencement ceremonies. Only those undergraduates who apply for fall are eligible to participate in the corresponding fall undergraduate commencement ceremony and are not eligible to participate in any other future commencement ceremonies.

When will I receive my diploma?

Diplomas are typically mailed approximately 8-10 weeks or so after the official graduation date. Please note that diplomas will be shipped via the United States Postal Service and should arrive at the provided address within 4-6 weeks of mailing if it is being delivered within the U.S. Please allow up to ten weeks for international shipments.

Can I change my diploma address after submitting my application?

If you need to change your diploma address after submitting your graduation application, please contact reghelp@uga.edu from your UGA email address and provide your student ID number (81X).

How can I change my diploma name information?

Diplomas are printed using the student's legal name on file with the University of Georgia. If a student needs to officially change his or her legal name with UGA, a Name Change Form needs to be submitted to the Office of the Registrar along with the required documentation.

I am getting a “No Curricula Available” error, what do I do?

Select a different Curriculum Term (Step 4 above) and try again. If the error persists, please contact reghelp@uga.edu.
