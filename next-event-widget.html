Next Event Widget

Embeddable calendar date components for WordPress

Card Style - Clean

Shows "Fall 2025 Grades Due (12 PM)" with no subtext line.

<!-- HTML embed (no subtext) --> <div data-next-event="grades-due" data-subtext=""></div> <!-- WordPress Shortcode --> [next_event type="grades-due" subtext=""]

Card Style - Custom Subtext

Card with custom subtext message.

<!-- HTML embed (custom subtext) --> <div data-next-event="classes-begin" data-subtext="All students should check Athena for their schedule."></div> <!-- WordPress Shortcode --> [next_event type="classes-begin" subtext="All students should check Athena for their schedule."]

Filter by Session

Get the next event for a specific session (FALL, SPRING, SSI, SSII, MAY, EXT, THRU).

<!-- HTML embed (FALL session only) --> <div data-next-event="withdrawal-deadline" data-session="FALL" data-subtext=""></div> <!-- WordPress Shortcode --> [next_event type="withdrawal-deadline" session="FALL" subtext=""]

Inline Style

Compact leaf that fits within a sentence. Perfect for body text.

Fall 2025 grades are due

...
-- ---
at 12:00 PM.

Spring 2026 classes begin on

...
-- ---
for all students.

<!-- WordPress Shortcode --> Fall 2025 grades are due [next_event type="grades-due" style="inline"] at 12:00 PM. <!-- Or HTML embed --> Fall 2025 grades are due <span data-next-event="grades-due" data-style="inline"></span> at 12:00 PM.

Multiple Events

Display several upcoming events together.

<!-- WordPress Shortcode --> [next_events types="classes-begin,withdrawal-deadline,final-exams" style="card"]
