Grades & Grading

The Office of the University Registrar maintains the official academic record for every student and supports faculty throughout each term. This page explains how grading works, when grades are due, and where to find step-by-step guidance.

Use the deadlines below to stay on schedule, follow the guides for detailed instructions, and reach out to our office if you have a question that isn't answered here.

About Grading

Instructors submit grades through Athena at the close of each part of term. Grades become part of the permanent academic record once they are rolled to history, typically within 48 hours of the submission deadline.

If you believe a grade was recorded in error, contact your instructor first. Formal grade changes are processed by the department and reviewed by the Registrar's Office before posting.
